## Name

MacBook Air M2 — a modern ultrabook by Apple released in 2022.

---

## Description

MacBook Air M2 is a lightweight laptop powered by the Apple M2 chip, featuring a Liquid Retina display  
and up to 18 hours of battery life. It combines thin design, high performance, and portability,  
making it perfect for study, work, and travel.

---

## Specifications

- Dimensions: 30.41 × 21.5 × 1.13 cm  
- Weight: 1.24 kg  
- Chip: Apple M2  
- Memory: up to 24 GB  
- SSD: up to 2 TB  
- Display: 13.6" Liquid Retina  
- Battery: up to 18 h
